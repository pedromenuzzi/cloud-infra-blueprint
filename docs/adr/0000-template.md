## Context

What is the problem we are trying to solve? What constraints (technical,
product, team) are in play right now? Why is this decision happening _now_
and not earlier or later?

Keep this section evidence-driven: link to issues, benchmarks, prior PRs and
the relevant files in the repo. Avoid persuasive language — the goal is to
let a reader 18 months from now reconstruct what we knew at decision time.

## Decision

The decision in **one declarative sentence**, then a paragraph or two of
detail. Prefer concrete artefacts (file paths, package names, function
signatures) over abstract principles.

## Alternatives considered

For each realistic alternative, one short paragraph covering:

- what the alternative would look like in practice;
- why it was not chosen (cost, risk, fit, team familiarity, license).

Listing alternatives explicitly prevents the same debate from being reopened
every six months.

## Consequences

What becomes easier and harder once this decision is in place. Be honest about
the downsides — every decision trades something off.

- **Positive:** _shorter feedback loops, smaller bundle, easier testing, …_
- **Negative:** _new operational footgun, vendor coupling, perf cliff, …_
- **Follow-ups:** _ADRs that this one will probably trigger, or open issues
  that should be created._
